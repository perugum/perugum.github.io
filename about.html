About Us

What We Do

Vayusoft Solutions Inc is an IT staffing services provider placing highly qualified IT professionals with companies and organizations nationwide. We offer a broad range of staffing options and solutions, encompassing most IT department requirements. Software programming and application development can be outsourced directly to us or handled by one of our associates overseas. Founded by a team of young, dynamic and task-oriented IT professionals, Vayu soft Inc., brings a pragmatic approach with proven, real-world solutions to the challenging fields of technology and IT personnel placement.

Vayusoft allows companies to improve corporate performance by enabling key elements in the managerial aspects of business. Vayusoft has proven competence in verticals such as Health Care, Baking, Insurance, Mortgage and Financials. Vayusoft has both the expertise and bandwidth to offer clients a wide array of development options and end-to-end solutions-on a global scale, extensively as well as intensively. A strong framework of processes, tools and techniques, implemented through well-defined and robust framework of procedures form the basis of all delivery activities at vayusoft leads to value addition in quality and productivity of the client's organization. Vayusoft Infosys Inc has an elite workforce of IT professionals on staff. Our objective has been to recruit only the highest caliber and most knowledgeable IT professionals available. Every employee is carefully selected, interviewed and tested before they can join your team. We have technology professionals from all over the world and more continuously hiring new, highly qualified individuals.
